            Q1. We are interested in retrieving only the students' names; all the names should be in caps.

            ['JOHN', 'BABA', 'YAGA', 'WICK']
        


        Solution:
        let arr = studentRecords.map((item)=>item.name.toUpperCase())
        console.log(arr)
